BANK HOLIDAY IN PAKISTAN

Bank Holiday in Pakistan in 2022 The State Bank of Pakistan will remain closed for public dealings on Monday July 6th 2020, which will be observed as a bank holiday. All banks, development finance institutions, microfinance banks will remain closed for public dealings on the aforesaid date. However, all employees of the banks, DFIs and MFBs will attend the office as usual. Each year, several days are announced as bank holidays in Pakistan. Banks remain closed on these days for the public, but not for employees who will have to report to work as usual. The bank holidays correspond to New Year's Day, the first day of Ramadan and half-year closing at the start of July.
